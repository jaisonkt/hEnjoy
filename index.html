The Difference

Free-range vs. cage-free

Free-range means outdoor access and natural behaviors. Cage-free means indoor-only. We champion free-range for healthier hens and better eggs.

Cage-Free

Hens live indoors without cages, but no access to the outdoors.

  • Indoor-only barns
  • Limited natural behaviors
  • Less sunshine and roaming

Free-Range

Hens have room to roam outside, forage, and soak up sunshine.

  • Outdoor access daily
  • Natural behaviors encouraged
  • Happier hens, better eggs
